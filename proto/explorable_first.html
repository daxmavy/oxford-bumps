Why these odds are worth reading on Friday

On Day 1 a single bump is barely better than a coin toss to call (AUC 0.54). By Friday it’s 0.82 — not because crews build momentum, but because four days of racing have revealed who is actually fast. The board sharpens as the week goes on.

Reading the factor bars

Each matchup’s odds are a sum of pieces. Form so far this week is the heaviest by far (×3.3 per standard deviation) — it’s the live read on speed. Last year’s finish, Torpids form, squad continuity and Blues in the boat add smaller, steadier nudges. Green pushes a bump toward likely; red pulls it back.
